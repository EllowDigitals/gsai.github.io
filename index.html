About Us

Ghatak Sports Academy India™ is a GOVT. & ISO 9001:2015 CERTIFIED INSTITUTE committed to excellence in martial arts and self-defense. We blend time-honored martial arts traditions with cutting-edge training methodologies to help individuals unlock their full potential—physically, mentally, and ethically.

At GSAI, we foster a disciplined and respectful learning environment where students are empowered to grow with strength, confidence, and integrity.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through comprehensive physical and mental training that builds resilience, discipline, and self-confidence.

Our Vision

To cultivate a strong, honorable, and resilient community prepared to face life’s challenges with courage and determination.
