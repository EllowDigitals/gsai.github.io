About Us

Ghatak Sports Academy India (GSAI) is dedicated to excellence in martial arts and self-defense. We combine traditional techniques with modern training to help you achieve your full potential in a disciplined, respectful environment.

Our Mission

Empowering individuals through comprehensive physical and mental training.

Our Vision

Yes! We provide trial sessions for new students. Reach out to us to book your trial class.

We train athletes of all ages, from children to adults.

Yes, we offer hostel accommodations for outstation students and athletes.

We provide training in multiple sports, including Cricket, Kabaddi, and more.

You can register online through our website or visit our academy for in-person enrollment.
